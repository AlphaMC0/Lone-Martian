# Mithril Gem

**Mithril** is a type of Gem.

# Obtaining

**Mithril Gems** are obtained from crafting together 5 **Mithril Shards**.

**5 Mithril Shards** ---> **1 Mithril Gem**

# Usage

**Mithril Gems** are refined into **Refined Mtihril Gems**.
